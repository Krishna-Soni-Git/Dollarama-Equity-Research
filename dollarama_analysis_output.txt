------------------------------------------------------------------------
  7.1  Why AI's formula was wrong
------------------------------------------------------------------------

  AI used:    ROIC = NOPAT / Shareholders' Equity
  Problem:    FY2022 equity = −$66M (buybacks exceeded retained earnings)
              NOPAT / negative equity = negative ROIC = MEANINGLESS

  Correct:    ROIC = EBIT × (1 − tax) / (Total Assets − Current Liabilities − Cash)
              Uses asset side of balance sheet — always positive
              Appropriate for companies with negative book equity
